
 
# Non-Conventional Energy Resources: A Review of the Book by S. Hasan Saeed
  
Non-conventional energy resources are those that do not rely on fossil fuels or nuclear power, such as solar, wind, geothermal, biomass, and ocean energy. These resources are becoming more important as the world faces the challenges of climate change, energy security, and environmental sustainability.
  
One of the books that covers this topic in detail is Non-conventional Energy Resources by S. Hasan Saeed and D. K. Sharma. This book was published in 2012 by S.K. Kataria & Sons and has 314 pages. It is intended for students and professionals who want to learn about the principles, applications, and challenges of non-conventional energy resources.
